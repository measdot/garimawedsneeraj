How to Reach

By Road

Hotel Galaxy is 231 kms from Rewa. The preferred route to take is : Take Padra Rd and Bankuiya Rd/Satna Maihar Rd to NH7 in Chorhata, Babupur then Follow NH7 to Amkhera Rd in Khirkiya Khurd,Continue on Amkhera Rd. Take Raddi Chowki Rd and MP SH 37A to Station Rd in Shukla Nagar, Jabalpur

Rail

Rewa Jabalpur Express: Departs from Rewa Station at 0600 hrs and arrives at Madanmahel at 0530 hrs. It is another 2 Kms by taxi to Hotel Galaxy.
